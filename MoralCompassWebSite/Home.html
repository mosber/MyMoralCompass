The Moral Compass Scorecard

 

Welcome to the Moral Compass Scorecard!

This website is a prototype of a site I am developing based on a series of articles I’ve been working on over the past year.  The topics are events in our history where people have made moral choices, and we (with the benefit of hindsight) get to give them a report card.

Each topic contains a presentation of the event with my article, my scorecard, and a number of videos, graphics and links available on the internet.  Some of the events are well known, such as the civil rights movement and the Viet Nam war.  Some are not so well known such as the McMartin trial.  Eventually, the site will be interactive, allowing visitors to add their own comments, create their own scorecards, and vote on a series of online polls relating to the events.

This prototype site only contains 2 live sections – the Anti-Communist blacklisting section and the Willie Horton Ads section.  The design and content are still in development.
